Walking and Running Force Plate Analysis

This script automatically runs through 12 different named force plate data files isolating the stance phase, and max force values to create 12 plots. Lastly creates a box plot to show the forces produced at different step lengths. I will only show 5 plots as they convey the product of the code.

This script runs through a folder of force plate files of:

  • 3 participants
  • Each who performed walking trials at 90% and 100% preferred step length
  • Running trials at 90% and 100% preferred step length.
  • In total 12 files of force plate data
